Dress Me Slowly

We've all heard it before: "Fashion industry is one of the biggest polluters and definitely no friend to the environment". It doesn't apply to slow fashion, though. At Buena Onda, we respect nature, the people, and the craft. While other brands are bombarding the market with one collection after the other, we allow ourselves to slow down, breathe and let the inspiration strike at the right moment.


Following the business model we invented, every collection consists of mere three items and is a celebration of the summer kick-off. At Buena Onda, we are not trying to keep up with the trends. We are setting our own by redefining the word "slow".


Take your time dressing up - be slow, be ethical, be cool! It never gets out of style.
